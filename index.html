History of the Argo Ship

Argo was constucted by the shipwrigt Argus,
and its crew were specially protected by the goddess Hera.
According to a variety of sources of the legend,
Argo was said to have been planned or constructed
with the help of Athena. According to certain
sources, Argo was the first ship to sail the seas.
